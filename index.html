Welcome to Bast Space

Amman's vintage sanctuary for rescued cats, coffee lovers, and cozy vibes.

More Than Just a Cafe

Located in the heart of Amman, Bast Space is a unique project founded by a veterinarian with a passion for animal welfare. It serves as a sanctuary where rescued cats are rehabilitated, loved, and given a safe space to thrive.

Whether you're looking to study in a vintage atmosphere, enjoy a delicious iced latte, or find your new best friend through adoption, our doors (and paws) are open.

"A place to relax, unwind, and experience the healing power of feline companionship."
